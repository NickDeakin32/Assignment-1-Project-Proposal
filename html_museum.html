Welcome to the Cars of Yesteryear!

At this Melbourne Car Museum we showcase a range of exotic and special cars spanning from the classics of the 1960s till the modern day masterpieces designed today!

A small part of our expansive collection of vehicles includes:

  • 1965 Ford Mustang
  • 1960 Austin Mini
  • 1993 BMW E30 3 Series
  • 1998 Nissan Skyline R34 GTR
  • 1994 Toyota Supra

Let us take you on a journey to discover the many wonders of automotive design spanning the past 60 years!

Expand your knowledge about motoring history with informative information about the history of cars, makes and models!

Experience our virtual tour showcasing some of our most prized possessions free of charge!
